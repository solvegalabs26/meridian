3. Sweep Credit System

Sweep credits let any user purchase additional on-demand sweeps beyond their plan's included cadence. One credit = one full sweep across all active objectives. Credits never expire and are available on all tiers including Trial.

Why credits work

A Trial user who isn't ready to subscribe can buy 5 credits for $4 and keep using the product. An Explorer user tracking a time-sensitive job window can burn 3 credits in one week when the hiring announcement drops. This usage pattern tells you exactly who your power users are before they upgrade — it's the most honest upgrade signal in the product.

5. Enterprise — Archived · Target Q2 2027

Archived — Not in current product scope

Enterprise is a separate revenue channel requiring different legal terms, sales motion, pricing structure, and product components. It is not appropriate to build alongside the self-serve subscription business at this stage. Target: Q2 2027 after self-serve is profitable and a technical co-founder or enterprise sales resource is engaged.

What Enterprise actually means (for when the time comes)

Two distinct enterprise models exist and should not be conflated:

ModelDescriptionTarget customerPricing modelDependencies
Option A — Team accounts Multiple users under one org billing. Same product, multi-seat management layer. Example: PE firm with 4 partners each tracking portfolio objectives. Small teams, family offices, professional partnerships $299–499/month for team seats Teams/orgs table, seat management, shared billing admin, optional shared objective visibility
Option B — White-label / API licensing Meridian's POS engine embedded in another company's product. Example: financial advisory firm offering POS tracking to clients, HR platform adding objective state to employee development tools. Financial advisors, HR platforms, insurance, wellness platforms $25k–250k/year annual contract Dedicated API, white-label theming, custom data isolation, enterprise SSO (SAML/OKTA), audit logs, dedicated infrastructure, MSA, DPA, SLA with financial penalties

Q2 2027 Enterprise project list

Captured for future build — not current scope

When Enterprise build begins in Q2 2027, these are the workstreams:

  • Master Services Agreement (MSA) template — requires attorney review, separate from consumer T&C
  • Data Processing Agreement (DPA) — required for any B2B data handling, GDPR compliance if international clients
  • SLA with defined uptime guarantees and financial penalties — requires dedicated infrastructure
  • Enterprise SSO — SAML/OKTA integration for corporate identity management
  • Audit logs — every data access event logged for compliance review
  • Custom data isolation — each enterprise client's data in isolated schema or database
  • White-label theming — client's brand on Meridian's engine
  • Dedicated customer success function — not founder-handled at enterprise scale
  • Enterprise sales motion — outbound, procurement cycles, security questionnaires
  • Pricing strategy — annual contract minimums, per-seat vs per-API-call vs flat fee

6. Disclaimer System

Meridian's AI output — confidence scores, action recommendations, signal analysis, cross-dependency detections — must always appear alongside a clear, consistent, non-intrusive disclaimer. The goal is normalized presence, not legal wallpaper. Users who see it everywhere stop reading it as a warning and start reading it as part of the product's honest identity.

Where the disclaimer appears

🔴 Onboarding — profile screen
/onboarding/profile
User is about to enter personal life data. Sets expectations before any AI output is generated. Highest-stakes consent moment.
🔴 Subscription / plan screen
/onboarding/plan
User is making a financial commitment based in part on product value claims. Disclaimer must appear before payment.
🔴 First sweep reveal
/onboarding/sweep
First AI output the user sees. They're most likely to over-weight this score. Disclaimer appears directly below the confidence number before they proceed to dashboard.
🔴 Objective scorecard
/objectives/[id]
Where AI confidence scores and action recommendations are most concentrated and most likely to influence real decisions. Single-line below action list.
Mission Control dashboard
/dashboard
Persistent footer line. Seen on every session. Normalizes disclaimer as part of the product identity rather than a one-time modal.
Email alerts
Resend transactional emails
Every confidence change alert email. Footer below unsubscribe link. User may act on email before returning to app.
Signal feed
/signals
One-line persistent footer. Signals are third-party sourced — accuracy of underlying information is outside Meridian's control.
Terms of Service
/legal/terms
Full legal language — all clauses including limitation of liability, not-a-professional-advisor, and AI accuracy disclaimer. Attorney reviewed before first paying user.
Privacy Policy
/legal/privacy
Anthropic API data flow disclosure required here. User inputs are processed by a third-party AI provider.
